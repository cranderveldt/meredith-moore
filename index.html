Biography

I am an experienced science communicator, policy analyst, and researcher with a passion for physical and environmental sciences. For over eight years, I’ve worked to enhance and protect the role of science in U.S. federal policy.

Underpinned by my graduate education in physics and astronomy and my training as a science writer, I break down complicated science and policy into accessible terms for stakeholders, elected officials, and the general public. I believe that with a strong foundation in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, we will be able to make the right decisions to ensure a sustainable, healthy world for all.
